üìã Quick Setup Steps

Step 1: Open the Configuration File

Open config.js in your favorite text editor (VS Code, Sublime Text, etc.)

Step 2: Replace Placeholder Information

Look for all text that starts with YOUR_ and replace with your actual information:

  • YOUR_FULL_NAME ‚Üí Your real name
  • your.email@domain.com ‚Üí Your email address
  • YOUR_JOB_TITLE ‚Üí Your job title/profession
  • X+ ‚Üí Your years of experience (e.g., "5+")
  • YOUR_PROFESSIONAL_SUMMARY... ‚Üí Write your professional summary

Step 3: Add Your Work Experience

In the workHistory section, replace the example jobs with your actual work experience. You can add or remove positions by copying the job object format.

Step 4: Update Skills & Languages

Modify the skills and languages arrays with your actual skills and language proficiencies.

Step 5: Choose Your Theme

Change the theme.preset value to one of:

  • 'blue' - Professional blue (default)
  • 'green' - Nature-inspired green
  • 'purple' - Creative purple
  • 'orange' - Energetic orange
  • 'red' - Bold red

Step 6: Configure Footer

Update the footer.github value with your GitHub username (without the @ symbol)
